White Pasta

Description

Pasta is typically a noodle made from an unleavened dough of a durum wheat flour mixed with water or eggs and formed into sheets or various shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Time

25 minutes

Serves

3

Ingredients

  1. ¾ cup pasta
  2. 1 small carrot, peeled and chopped fine
  3. ⅓ cup sweet corn kernels, boiled
  4. ½ green bell pepper, chopped fine
  5. ½ red bell pepper,chopped fine
  6. ½ tablespoon butter
  7. For white sauce
  8. 1½ tablespoons butter
  9. 1 tablespoon maida/ all purpose flour
  10. 1¼ cup milk, boiled and cooled
  11. ½ teaspoon pepper powder
  12. ¼ teaspoon dried oregano or basil or thyme
  13. ½ teaspoon chilli flakes
  14. Salt

Directions

  1. Boil the pasta and set aside.
  2. Heat ½ tablespoon butter in a pan and saute the chopped vegetables. Remove to a plate.
  3. Add 1½ tablespoons butter in the same pan and add maida/flour. Whisk very well.
  4. Add boiled milk and mix well. Whisk very well in low flame to avoid lumps.
  5. Once the sauce starts to thicken, add salt, pepper powder, chilli flakes and dried herbs. Mix well.
  6. Cook further in low flame till the sauce thickens and turns smooth.
  7. Add sauteed veggies and mix well.
  8. Add boiled pasta and mix well.
  9. Serve white sauce pasta hot.
