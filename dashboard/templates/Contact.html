What is Ava

Do you have only few ingredients at home and don't know how what to make?

Ava is a recipe search engine that finds recipes you can make with the ingredients you currently have at home according to your desire.

For best results, make sure to tell Ava about every ingredient you have at home. The more ingredients you add to Ava, the better the recipes will be!

Ava is also a practical way to save money. Take full advantage of ingredients you already have, and naturally buy less groceries.

How do I add my ingredients?

Adding your ingredients is done on the right side panel.

You can either type(list) your ingredients one by one, or you can use the category view. On category view, simply check off the ingredients you have on each category.

For best results, add all the ingredients you have in your home. The more ingredients you tell Ava you have, the better the recipes will be.

What are the benefits of signing up for an account?

If you sign up for an account, your ingredients, and dietary restrictions, will be saved on Ava Pantry.

Can I use Supercook without signing up?

Yes, but you won't be able to save your ingrdients or recipes for future use.
